# Phase 12 — Improvement

## Card acquisition improvement signals

Allow recommendations based on:

- Purchase pace
- Purchase conversion
- Purchased hand share
- Hand utilization
- End-hand carryover
- Cards seen relative to outcomes

Only generate recommendations when minimum sample and data-coverage
requirements are met.

Every recommendation must include:

- Supporting games
- Comparison baseline
- Eligible-game count
- Coverage level
- Uncertainty
- Observational, non-causal wording
